1. 启动zookeeper: zkServer.sh start
2. 启动kafka服务: kafka-server-start.sh config/server.properties
3. 发送方: kafka-console-producer.sh --broker-list localhost:9092 --topic test
4. 接收方: kafka-console-consumer.sh --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic test

关闭kafka: kafka-server-stop.sh
关闭zk: ./zkServer.sh stop
